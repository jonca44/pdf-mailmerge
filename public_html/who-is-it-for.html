Who Is It For?

Rocket Mail Merge is perfect for the professionals and businesses that need to send out many reminder letters or pre fill forms for their customers. For example if you run a hair salon, massage therapy practice, law firm, or auto repair business, Rocket Mail Merge will help you create both your direct mail marketing and reminder letters for your customers.


Rocket Mail Merge can also help you prepare letters for people you want to stay in contact with — for example you can make Christmas cards, wedding invites and the like. If you use it, your customers will be happier when they receive their letters or advertising addressed to them instead of 'Dear Sir or Madam' and you'll save plenty of time by not having to hand edit every letter.

  • Beauty Salons

  • Direct Mail Advertising & Marketing Companies
  • Lawyers and Law Firms
  • Florists
